Overview

Purpose

My website will be focused on tourism and recommendations for shops and restaurants to visit when travelling to a particular place. I will start the website off by providing recommendations when visiting three places in Italy- Venice, Florence, and Rome, with the promise for more places around the world to be added in the future. The purpose of the website will be to help people who are travelling to these places have an authentic and memorable experience when they visit.

Audience

This site will be for a general audience, with an emphasis on families who are travelling.
